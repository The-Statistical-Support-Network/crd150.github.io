CRD 150 - Quantitative Methods in Community Research

Fall 2018


Your final project involves studying an interesting question about a U.S. community. We define community as a metropolitan area, city, or county. You must select a metropolitan area, city, or county with a population size greater than 250,000. The eligible metro areas, cities, and counties using 2012-2016 American Community Survey estimates are shown below in order of population size.
